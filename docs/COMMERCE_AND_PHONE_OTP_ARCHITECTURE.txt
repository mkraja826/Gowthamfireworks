1. BUSINESS MODEL
-----------------
The website will serve two customer channels from one catalogue and one owner admin panel:

A. B2C — Factory to Customer
- Retail customers browse the public catalogue.
- Retail prices, retail pack sizes and customer availability are displayed.
- A verified phone login is required before submitting an order request.
- Customers can save addresses, review past requests and track status.

B. B2B — Factory to Wholesaler
- Wholesalers use the same phone OTP login.
- A new user applies for a wholesale account.
- The application includes business and licence details required by the owner.
- The owner reviews and approves or rejects the application.
- Only approved wholesale accounts can see wholesale prices, carton quantities, minimum quantities and wholesale-only products.
- Wholesale pricing may be assigned by tier or directly to an account.

2. COMPLIANCE-FIRST ORDERING RULE
---------------------------------
The application will technically support carts, order records, quotes, confirmations and status tracking.

Production behaviour must remain configurable:
- ENQUIRY_ONLY: customer submits an order/enquiry request; no online payment or completed online sale.
- MANUAL_CONFIRMATION: owner verifies customer, licence where required, location, stock, transport and legal eligibility before confirming.
- LICENSED_ORDERING: may only be enabled after the business obtains written legal/compliance confirmation for the exact operating model and locations.

Default launch mode: ENQUIRY_ONLY / MANUAL_CONFIRMATION.

No payment gateway, automatic dispatch promise or final online-sale wording is to be enabled merely because the software supports orders.

3. USER TYPES AND ACCESS
------------------------
- guest: browse published retail catalogue.
- retail_customer: verified phone user with retail access.
- wholesale_applicant: verified phone user whose wholesale application is pending.
- wholesaler: owner-approved business account with wholesale catalogue access.
- staff: optional restricted admin access added later.
- owner_admin: full catalogue, customer, wholesaler, pricing, order and settings access.

Roles must be enforced in Supabase Row Level Security, not only hidden in the user interface.

4. PHONE NUMBER AND OTP LOGIN
-----------------------------
Authentication provider: Supabase Auth phone OTP.

Login flow:
1. User selects India country code or enters an international phone number.
2. Application normalises the number to E.164 format, for example +919876543210.
3. CAPTCHA/anti-bot validation runs before OTP request.
4. Supabase requests a six-digit OTP through the configured SMS provider.
5. User enters the OTP.
6. Supabase verifies the OTP and creates a secure authenticated session.
7. A first-time user completes name and customer type selection.
8. Returning users are redirected to their account or previous destination.

Security requirements:
- OTP secrets are never stored in application tables.
- Rate-limit OTP requests by phone, IP and device signals.
- Resend timer and maximum verification attempts.
- Generic errors to prevent account enumeration.
- Audit login events without storing OTP values.
- Block abusive phone numbers and IP addresses.
- Use secure server-managed cookies for web sessions where supported.
- Owner/admin phone numbers must be pre-authorised in the database.
- Owner/admin access should add a second factor or recovery method before production.

India SMS requirement:
- Complete applicable TRAI DLT Principal Entity, sender header and OTP content-template registration.
- Use a supported Supabase provider or Supabase Send SMS Hook with a suitable Indian SMS provider.
- Keep provider credentials only in Supabase or hosting secrets.

5. RETAIL CUSTOMER FLOW
-----------------------
1. Browse catalogue without login.
2. Select retail quantities and add to cart.
3. See estimated total and product availability.
4. Login with phone OTP at checkout/order-request stage.
5. Add or select delivery/contact address.
6. Confirm age/legal acknowledgements and service-area conditions where required.
7. Submit order request.
8. System stores immutable product-name, price and quantity snapshots.
9. Owner checks stock, service area and compliance.
10. Customer receives confirmed amount and next steps by account status, SMS or WhatsApp.
11. Customer tracks the request in My Orders.

6. WHOLESALE CUSTOMER FLOW
--------------------------
1. Login using phone OTP.
2. Choose Apply for Wholesale Access.
3. Submit:
   - business/legal name;
   - contact person;
   - GSTIN where applicable;
   - fireworks/explosives sale or possession licence details where applicable;
   - licence expiry date;
   - shop and delivery addresses;
   - state and service area;
   - supporting documents;
   - expected purchase volume.
4. Application status becomes pending_review.
5. Owner reviews documents and may request correction.
6. Owner approves, rejects, suspends or expires the account.
7. Approved wholesaler sees wholesale prices, MOQ, carton quantity and B2B availability.
8. Wholesaler submits quote/order request.
9. Owner confirms price, stock, documentation, transport and terms.
10. All approval and status changes are audited.

7. PRICING MODEL
----------------
Each product can have channel-specific information:
- retail_price;
- retail_mrp;
- retail_min_quantity;
- wholesale_price;
- wholesale_moq;
- carton_quantity;
- wholesale_tier prices;
- retail availability;
- wholesale availability;
- retail visibility;
- wholesale visibility.

Recommended flexible model:
- price_lists table: Retail, Wholesale Standard, Wholesale Silver, Wholesale Gold, or custom account list.
- product_prices table: product, price list, unit price, minimum quantity, effective dates.
- business_accounts.price_list_id assigns the correct wholesale pricing.

Prices displayed to a logged-in wholesaler must come from the approved price list and must not be trusted from the browser during submission.

8. CART AND ORDER RULES
-----------------------
- One user can maintain separate retail and wholesale carts.
- Retail and wholesale items cannot be mixed in one order.
- Server recalculates current price and eligibility before submission.
- Out-of-stock and hidden products cannot be submitted.
- Quantity must follow pack size, MOQ and carton rules.
- Orders store product and price snapshots so historical orders never change when the catalogue changes.
- Inventory reservation occurs only after owner confirmation unless explicitly configured otherwise.

Suggested order statuses:
- draft
- submitted
- compliance_review
- awaiting_stock_confirmation
- quote_sent
- customer_accepted
- confirmed
- processing
- packed
- dispatched
- delivered
- rejected
- cancelled

Payment-related statuses are not enabled until the business flow is legally approved.

11. ROW LEVEL SECURITY SUMMARY
------------------------------
- Guests may read only published retail catalogue fields.
- Retail customers may read and change only their own profile, addresses, cart and orders.
- Wholesale applicants may read only their own application and its status.
- Approved wholesalers may read wholesale catalogue data assigned to their price list.
- A wholesaler can never read another wholesaler's pricing, documents or orders.
- Public users cannot read wholesale documents, customer phone numbers or admin data.
- Only owner/admin may change products, prices, approval status and order status.
- Service-role credentials never appear in browser code.

13. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
-----------------------
- A customer can authenticate with phone number and OTP.
- A guest cannot access account, wholesale or admin data.
- A retail user sees retail prices only.
- A wholesale applicant does not see wholesale prices before approval.
- An approved wholesaler sees only their assigned price list.
- The owner can manage photos, prices, MOQ, carton quantities and availability per channel.
- Retail and wholesale orders remain separated.
- Every submitted item stores an immutable price snapshot.
- Admin and customer data are protected by tested RLS policies.
- Production mode cannot silently turn an enquiry into an online sale.
