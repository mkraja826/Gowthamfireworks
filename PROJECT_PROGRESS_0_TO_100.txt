PROJECT GOAL
------------
Build a premium mobile-first Gowtham Fireworks Industries platform serving:
- B2C: factory to personal/retail customer.
- B2B: factory to approved business/wholesaler.

The owner must control products, photos, categories, retail and wholesale pricing, MOQ, carton quantities, availability, stock, customer requests, business applications, offers and website settings without editing code.

CONFIRMED PRODUCT DIRECTION
---------------------------
Status: APPROVED

1. Premium public landing page and searchable catalogue.
2. One application with separate Personal/B2C and Business/B2B paths.
3. Phone-number and OTP authentication.
4. Supabase Test OTP during development and staging.
5. First-time customer chooses Personal or Business after OTP verification.
6. Personal creates an active retail profile.
7. Business creates a business profile and pending wholesale application.
8. Wholesale prices remain hidden until owner approval.
9. Admin is never shown as a public account-type option.
10. Pre-authorised owner/admin users use /admin/login and route directly to /admin after role verification.
11. Normal customers cannot promote themselves to admin.
12. Owner controls every product photo, retail price, wholesale price, stock and availability from admin.
13. Dedicated Supabase project/account; never mix data with CapDent, Astro or another project.
14. Production ordering uses compliance-first configuration.
15. Default launch behaviour is enquiry/order-request plus manual owner confirmation.
16. No payment gateway is included in version one.
17. Visual direction is “Sivakasi Midnight Festival”.
18. Public UI is celebratory; admin UI is calm, light and operational.

COMMERCE AND COMPLIANCE MODE
----------------------------
Status: APPROVED ARCHITECTURE; LEGAL OPERATING REVIEW REQUIRED

Modes:
- ENQUIRY_ONLY: customer submits a requirement; no completed online sale.
- MANUAL_CONFIRMATION: owner checks customer, stock, location, licence where applicable, transport and eligibility.
- LICENSED_ORDERING: disabled until written legal/compliance approval for the exact model and regions.

Default launch mode:
- ENQUIRY_ONLY / MANUAL_CONFIRMATION

No payment gateway or automatic dispatch promise is enabled merely because the software supports carts and request records.
