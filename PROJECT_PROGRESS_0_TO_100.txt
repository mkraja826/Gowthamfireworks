PROJECT GOAL
------------
Build a premium mobile-first Gowtham Fireworks Industries platform serving:

- B2C: factory to personal/retail customer.
- B2B: factory to approved business/wholesaler.

The owner must control products, photos, categories, retail and wholesale pricing, MOQ, carton quantities, availability, stock, customer requests, business applications, orders and website settings without editing code.

CONFIRMED PRODUCT DIRECTION
---------------------------
Status: APPROVED

1. Premium public landing page and searchable catalogue.
2. One application with separate Personal/B2C and Business/B2B paths.
3. Phone-number and OTP authentication.
4. A first-time customer chooses Personal or Business only after successful OTP verification.
5. Personal creates an active retail profile.
6. Business creates a business profile and pending wholesale application.
7. Wholesale prices remain hidden until owner approval.
8. Admin is never shown as a public account-type choice.
9. Pre-authorised owner/admin users log in through /admin/login and go directly to /admin after role verification.
10. Normal customers cannot promote themselves to admin.
11. Owner controls every product photo, price and availability from a secure admin panel.
12. Dedicated Supabase account/project; never mix data with CapDent, Astro or another project.
13. Production ordering uses a compliance-first configuration.
14. Default launch behaviour is enquiry/order request plus manual owner confirmation.

AUTHENTICATION AND PROFILE ROUTING
----------------------------------
Status: APPROVED; NOT IMPLEMENTED

Customer route:
- /login
- Enter phone number and OTP.
- After OTP, server reads role and profile.
- Existing users go directly to their correct account area.
- New users choose Personal or Business.

First-time Personal result:
- profiles.account_type = personal
- role = retail_customer
- status = active
- retail access available immediately, subject to operating rules.

First-time Business result:
- profiles.account_type = business
- role = wholesale_applicant
- business status = pending_review
- wholesale pricing unavailable until owner approval.

Admin route:
- /admin/login
- Only pre-authorised owner_admin or staff identities are accepted.
- owner_admin redirects directly to /admin.
- staff redirects only to allowed admin sections.
- non-admin authenticated users are denied.
- no public Admin signup or Admin option exists.

Server routing priority:
1. owner_admin -> /admin
2. staff -> authorised admin area
3. existing personal -> /account or prior retail destination
4. approved business -> /wholesale
5. pending business -> /business/application-status
6. rejected/suspended/expired business -> relevant status page
7. no profile -> /onboarding/account-type

Detailed record:
- docs/COMMERCE_AND_PHONE_OTP_ARCHITECTURE.txt

COMMERCE AND COMPLIANCE MODE
----------------------------
Status: APPROVED ARCHITECTURE; LEGAL OPERATING REVIEW REQUIRED

- ENQUIRY_ONLY: customer submits a request; no completed online sale.
- MANUAL_CONFIRMATION: owner checks customer, location, licence where applicable, stock, transport and eligibility.
- LICENSED_ORDERING: disabled until written legal/compliance approval for the exact model and service regions.

Default launch mode:
- ENQUIRY_ONLY / MANUAL_CONFIRMATION

No payment gateway or automatic dispatch promise is enabled merely because the software supports carts and order records.

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------
- OTP values never stored in application tables or logs.
- Phone numbers normalised to E.164.
- CAPTCHA, resend timer, expiry handling and rate limits.
- Users cannot write their own admin role, approval status or price list.
- Owner/admin role assigned only through protected operations.
- Retail users access only their own profile, address, cart and orders.
- Businesses access only their own application, documents and orders.
- Approved wholesalers see only assigned wholesale pricing.
- Wholesale documents remain private.
- Browser-supplied roles, prices and approval status are never trusted.
- Service-role credentials never appear in browser code.
- High-risk admin actions require recent authentication and audit logging.
