PROJECT GOAL
------------
Build a premium, mobile-first Gowtham Fireworks Industries website serving both:

- B2C: factory to retail customer.
- B2B: factory to approved wholesaler.

The website will include a secure owner-controlled admin panel. The owner must manage the website without editing code, including:

- Product names and descriptions.
- Product photographs.
- Categories and brands.
- Retail and wholesale prices.
- Retail and wholesale availability.
- MOQ and carton quantities.
- Stock and low-stock status.
- Featured products and combo packs.
- Retail customer requests/orders.
- Wholesale applications and requests/orders.
- Business, contact and website settings.

COMMERCE AND COMPLIANCE MODE
----------------------------
Status: APPROVED ARCHITECTURE; LEGAL OPERATING REVIEW STILL REQUIRED

The software will support carts, order records, quotes, confirmations and status tracking. Production behaviour will be controlled by a site setting:

- ENQUIRY_ONLY: customers submit an enquiry/order request; no online payment or completed online sale.
- MANUAL_CONFIRMATION: owner verifies stock, customer, location, licences where applicable, transport and legal eligibility before confirmation.
- LICENSED_ORDERING: can only be enabled after written legal/compliance approval for the exact business model and service locations.

Default launch mode:
- ENQUIRY_ONLY / MANUAL_CONFIRMATION.

No payment gateway, automatic dispatch promise or final online-sale wording will be enabled simply because the application technically supports orders.

PHONE OTP AUTHENTICATION PLAN
-----------------------------
Status: APPROVED; NOT IMPLEMENTED

Customer flow:
1. Enter phone number.
2. Normalise to E.164 format, such as +919876543210.
3. Run CAPTCHA/anti-bot validation.
4. Request six-digit OTP through Supabase Auth.
5. Verify OTP.
6. Create secure authenticated session.
7. First-time user completes profile and chooses retail or wholesale application.
8. Returning user continues to account or previous destination.

Security requirements:
- Never store OTP values in project tables or logs.
- Rate-limit by phone number, IP and device signals.
- Add resend timer and maximum verification attempts.
- Use generic authentication errors.
- Audit login events without storing secrets.
- Pre-authorise owner/admin phone numbers.
- Add an additional recovery or second-factor method for production admin access.
- Store provider credentials only in Supabase or hosting secrets.

B2C — FACTORY TO CUSTOMER FLOW
------------------------------
Status: APPROVED; NOT IMPLEMENTED

1. Guest browses the retail catalogue.
2. Customer adds retail items to a retail cart.
3. Website displays estimated value and current availability.
4. Phone OTP login is required before submission.
5. Customer selects or adds address and contact details.
6. Customer accepts required safety, age, location and service conditions.
7. Customer submits an order/enquiry request.
8. Server stores immutable product-name, price and quantity snapshots.
9. Owner checks stock, service area and compliance.
10. Owner confirms, revises or rejects the request.
11. Customer tracks status in My Orders.

B2B — FACTORY TO WHOLESALER FLOW
--------------------------------
Status: APPROVED; NOT IMPLEMENTED

1. User logs in using phone OTP.
2. User chooses Apply for Wholesale Access.
3. Application collects business name, contact person, GSTIN where applicable, licence details where applicable, expiry date, addresses, state, expected purchase volume and supporting documents.
4. Application status becomes pending_review.
5. Owner reviews and may request correction.
6. Owner approves, rejects, suspends or expires access.
7. Approved wholesaler receives an assigned wholesale price list.
8. Only approved wholesalers can see wholesale prices, MOQ, carton quantities and wholesale-only availability.
9. Wholesaler submits quote/order request.
10. Owner confirms price, stock, documentation, transport and terms.
11. Approval and status changes are recorded in audit history.

PRICING AND CART RULES
----------------------
Status: APPROVED; NOT IMPLEMENTED

- Retail and wholesale items cannot be mixed in one cart or order.
- A user may maintain separate retail and wholesale carts.
- Retail users see retail pricing only.
- Wholesale applicants do not see wholesale pricing until approved.
- Approved wholesalers see only their assigned price list.
- Server recalculates price and eligibility before submission.
- Browser-submitted prices are never trusted.
- Out-of-stock, hidden or ineligible products cannot be submitted.
- Quantities must follow pack, MOQ and carton rules.
- Orders preserve product and price snapshots.
- Inventory is reserved only after owner confirmation unless explicitly configured otherwise.
