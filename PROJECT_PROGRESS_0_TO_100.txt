- Backend base URL is configured by the FlashBillr deployment.
- Axios appends /api to the backend base URL.
- Store-admin product endpoint: GET /api/storeadmin/products.
- Profile endpoint: GET /api/auth/profile.

- Product response wrapper: products plus pagination.
- Pagination fields: page, limit, total and pages.
- Product fields: id, name, description, category, brand, sku, mrp, currentStock, contentType, sellingPrice, gstRate, youtubeUrl, images and createdAt.
